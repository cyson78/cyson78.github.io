About Me:

I am an Implementation Analyst for a software company catering to the hospitality industry. I have a Masters in Technology Management and hold about 2.5 years in management experience for teams as small as 5 members to as large as 20 members. My leadership style is empowering those to stand on their own two feet, to trust not just the person but their decision and to keep an open environment where mistakes are learning experiences and discussions are welcomed. I don't believe in micro-managing or making decisions by myself. Everyone has their good and bad days and I believe in trusting in the person, not the resume.
